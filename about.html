About Us

Our Mission

in 1983 in Atlanta, Georgia, the restaurant began with one man's passion for perfect steak. By 1990, the restaurant had spread throughout the East, Midwest and Southwest United States and Puerto Rico. In 2007, the restaurant, now called American Steakhouse, was purchased by Darden Restaurants, Inc.

Our Essence

We strive to be the best steakhouse in everything we do. One visit and you'll experience our grill master's Legendary Steaks like our center-cut Flo's Filet or Prime Rib.

Our Promise

The restaurant bases its philosophy on ‘Its All About The Food’ and pays homage to the very best beef from not only around the world but also our own shores. We have procured the best meats internationally.

Our Vibe

Premium, Fancy and Family-Friendly. Those are just some words that describe our restaurant.
